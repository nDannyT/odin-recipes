Milk Steak

Nasty Milk Steak

The best thing I've ever eaten!! - Review by Charlie

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Milk
  • Jelly Beans

Steps

  1. Cook the steak on a radiator so it doesn't burn
  2. Dump milk onto it
  3. Top it off with some jelly beans to your liking
  4. Toss it all in the trash
